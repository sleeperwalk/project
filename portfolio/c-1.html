Areas of Computer Science

Cybersecurity: This area of Computer Science keeps personal and business data safe from hackers and cyber threats. It ensures trust in digital systems and protects privacy in an online world.

Artifical Intelligence: This allows machines to make decisions and solve problems. It improves efficiency in areas such as healthcare, business and technology, but brings about ethical and privacy issues.

Data Science: helps analyze vast amounts of information to uncover patterns and trends. It supports better decision-making in businesses, healthcare, and research, positively impacting society.

Software Engineering: develops apps and programs we use every day. It enhances productivity, connects people worldwide, and drives innovation, making modern technology reliable and accessible.
